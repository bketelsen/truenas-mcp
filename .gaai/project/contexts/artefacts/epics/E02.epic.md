# Epic: Storage Visibility

## Purpose

An AI assistant can query and understand the complete storage state of the NAS — pools, datasets, and snapshots — enabling informed decisions about storage management.

---

## Scope

- List and inspect ZFS pools
- List and inspect datasets with properties (used, available, compression, etc.)
- List and inspect snapshots with metadata

---

## Out of Scope

- Creating, modifying, or deleting storage resources (handled by E03)
- Replication tasks

---

## Stories

> Stories are tracked in the backlog. This list is descriptive only.

- E02S01: List and inspect pools
- E02S02: List and inspect datasets
- E02S03: List and inspect snapshots

---

## Success Metrics

- AI assistant can retrieve a full picture of storage state through MCP tools
